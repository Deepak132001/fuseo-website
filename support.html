Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account? ‚ñº

Download the Fuseo app from Google Play or the App Store. Register with your email, create a profile, select your category (Startup, Trading, Personal Development, or Influencer), and upload at least one photo. You'll need to enable location services for matching.

How does matching work? ‚ñº

Fuseo uses a smart algorithm to match you with compatible collaborators based on your category preferences, skills, goals, location, and compatibility scores. Swipe right to like, left to pass, or superlike for priority visibility. When both users swipe right, it's a match!

Is Fuseo free to use? ‚ñº

Yes! Fuseo is currently completely free to use with all features available to every user. We may introduce premium features in the future, but the core networking functionality will always be accessible.

How do I delete my account? ‚ñº

To delete your account, email us at support@fuseo.app with "Account Deletion Request" in the subject line. Include your registered email address. We'll process your request within 48 hours and delete your data within 30 days as per our Privacy Policy.

Can I change my category after registration? ‚ñº

Yes! You can change your category and update your preferences at any time through the app settings. Your matches will be updated to reflect your new category preferences.

How is my location data used? ‚ñº

Your location is used only for matching purposes. Other users see an approximate distance (e.g., "5 km away"), not your exact coordinates. You can set your preferred matching distance from 10km to Worldwide. For more details, see our Privacy Policy.

What should I do if I encounter inappropriate behavior? ‚ñº

Use the in-app report feature to report any violations of our Terms of Service. You can also block users directly. We take all reports seriously and will investigate each case. For urgent safety concerns, contact us immediately at support@fuseo.app.
