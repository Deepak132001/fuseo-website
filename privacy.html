# Privacy Policy for Fuseo

Last Updated: December 23, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Fuseo ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use the Fuseo mobile application (the "App"). Fuseo is a professional networking platform that connects users based on collaboration goals across four categories: Startup, Trading, Personal Development, and Influencer.

By using Fuseo, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

Account Information:
  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted and securely stored)
  • Full name
  • Date of birth
  • Gender
  • Bio/description
  • Profile photos (up to 6 photos)
Category-Specific Information:

Depending on your selected category, you may provide:

Startup Category:
  • Role (idea owner or builder)
  • Skills you need/have
  • Project stage
  • Time commitment
  • Industry focus
Trading Category:
  • Trading styles and strategies
  • Experience level
  • Risk tolerance
  • Trading platforms used
  • Available hours
  • Timezone preferences
Personal Development Category:
  • Development subcategory (physique, mental, spiritual)
  • Current goals
  • Experience level
  • Preferred approach/style
  • Availability
Influencer Category:
  • Type (wannabe, established, or growing)
  • Social media platforms
  • Niche/content category
  • Follower counts
  • Collaboration goals
Communication Data:
  • Messages you send through the App
  • Photos you share in chats
  • Group chat participation
  • Icebreaker responses
Safety and Moderation Data:
  • Reports You Submit: When you report a user, profile, or message for violating our Terms of Service
  • Reports About You: Reports filed against your account by other users
  • Blocking Data: Information about users you have blocked and users who have blocked you
  • Moderation Actions: Warnings, suspensions, or enforcement actions taken on your account
  • Investigation Data: Information collected during safety investigations

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

Location Information:
  • Precise geographic location (latitude and longitude) when you grant location permissions
  • We use your location to show you potential matches within your preferred distance range
  • Location data is used for matching purposes only and is not shared publicly
Device Information:
  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers
  • Mobile network information
  • Push notification tokens (for sending notifications)
Usage Information:
  • Swipe history (left, right, superlike)
  • Matches made
  • Messages sent and received
  • App features used
  • Login dates and times
  • In-app activity patterns
Technical Information:
  • IP address
  • Browser type (for web access)
  • Connection information
  • Error logs and crash reports

1.3 Information from Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following third-party services that may collect information:

Cloudinary:
  • Stores and processes your profile photos and chat images
  • Images are stored securely on Cloudinary's cloud infrastructure
Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM):
  • Manages push notifications
  • Collects device tokens for notification delivery
Expo Push Notification Service:
  • Facilitates push notification delivery
  • Processes notification delivery receipts
Nominatim/OpenStreetMap:
  • Provides geocoding and reverse geocoding services
  • Processes location data for address lookup

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

2.1 Core App Functionality

  • Create and manage your account
  • Enable profile creation and customization
  • Facilitate the matching algorithm based on category-specific criteria
  • Calculate compatibility scores between users
  • Filter potential matches by location and distance preferences
  • Enable swiping functionality (like, pass, superlike)
  • Facilitate matches between compatible users
  • Enable real-time chat messaging (text and photos)
  • Create and manage group chats
  • Deliver icebreaker questions to start conversations

2.2 Communication

  • Send you push notifications about new matches
  • Notify you of new messages
  • Alert you to important account updates
  • Send you icebreaker question suggestions
  • Provide customer support responses

2.3 Safety and Security

  • Verify your identity and prevent fraud
  • Detect and prevent spam, abuse, and other harmful activities
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and Community Guidelines
  • Protect against unauthorized access
  • Monitor for security threats
  • Investigate and respond to user reports within 24 hours
  • Process blocking requests and maintain blocked user lists
  • Remove objectionable content and enforce zero tolerance policies
  • Track moderation actions to identify repeat violators
  • Prevent banned users from creating new accounts

2.4 Improvement and Analytics

  • Analyze app usage patterns to improve features
  • Understand user preferences and behavior
  • Test new features and functionality
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Optimize matching algorithms
  • Enhance user experience

2.5 Legal Compliance

  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Respond to legal requests and court orders
  • Protect our legal rights and interests
  • Enforce our agreements

  • Respond to claims of illegal activity or violations of third-party rights
  • Investigate reported violations and enforce our zero tolerance policies

3.4 Business Transfers

If Fuseo is involved in a merger, acquisition, bankruptcy, dissolution, reorganization, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change and inform you of choices you may have regarding your information.

3.5 With Your Consent

We may share your information for other purposes with your explicit consent.

4. Your Rights and Choices

  • Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
Right to Rectification:
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"):
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • See Section 5 below for account deletion process
Right to Restrict Processing:
  • Request that we limit how we use your information
Right to Data Portability:
  • Request your data in a structured, commonly used format
Right to Object:
  • Object to our processing of your personal information
Right to Withdraw Consent:
  • Withdraw consent at any time where we rely on consent to process your data
Right to Lodge a Complaint:
  • File a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@fuseo.site or support@fuseo.site.

4.2 All Users

Account Settings:
  • Edit your profile information at any time through the App
  • Change your category preferences
  • Update your location and distance preferences
  • Manage your photos (add, delete, set profile picture)
  • Change your password
Location Permissions:
  • Control location access through your device settings

Push Notifications:
  • Manage notification preferences through your device settings
  • Disable notifications at any time (may affect user experience)
Chat and Communication:
  • Delete messages you've sent (only from your view)

  • Block or report users using in-app tools

5. Account Deletion

YOU CAN DELETE YOUR ACCOUNT AND ALL ASSOCIATED DATA AT ANY TIME.

5.1 How to Delete Your Account

Fuseo provides straightforward account deletion:

Option 1: In-App Deletion (Coming Soon)
  • Navigate to Settings > Account > Delete Account
  • Confirm your decision
  • Your account will be scheduled for permanent deletion
Option 2: Email Request (Current Method)
  • Send an email to support@fuseo.site
  • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  • Include your registered email address in the message body
  • We will confirm receipt within 24 hours
  • Your account will be processed for deletion within 48 hours

5.2 What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

Immediate Removal (within 48 hours):
  • Your profile becomes invisible to all other users
  • You lose access to your account, matches, and messages
  • Your profile information (name, bio, photos)
  • All chat messages and conversation history
  • Your swipe history and match data
  • Location data and preferences
Complete Deletion (within 30 days):
  • All personal information from our database servers (Supabase)
  • All profile photos and chat images from cloud storage (Cloudinary)
  • Push notification tokens and device information
  • Account credentials and authentication data
  • Category preferences and profile settings
  • Usage history and analytics tied to your identity
  • Reports you filed and moderation data

5.3 Data Retention Exceptions

Some limited data may be retained after account deletion:

Backup Systems (up to 90 days):
  • Deleted data may persist in backup systems for up to 90 days
  • Backups are encrypted and not accessible for normal operations
  • Data in backups is automatically purged after 90 days
Legal and Safety Requirements:

We may retain certain information longer if required by law or for legitimate business purposes:

  • Fraud Prevention: Information about banned or suspended accounts may be retained to prevent re-registration by bad actors
  • Legal Compliance: Data required by law, court order, or regulatory requirement
  • Safety Investigations: Information relevant to ongoing safety or security investigations
  • Dispute Resolution: Data needed to resolve legal disputes or enforce our Terms
Anonymized Data:
  • Aggregated, anonymized analytics data (which cannot identify you personally) may be retained indefinitely
  • This data is used only for statistical analysis and app improvement

5.4 Effects on Other Users

When you delete your account:

  • Your profile disappears from other users' match lists and chat screens
  • Matched users will see that the conversation is no longer available
  • Messages you sent remain visible to recipients in their chat history
  • Other users cannot access your deleted profile or photos

5.5 No Account Recovery

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

  • Once deleted, you cannot recover your account, profile, or messages
  • You cannot reclaim your username or matches
  • If you wish to use Fuseo again, you must create a completely new account

5.6 Alternative to Deletion: Account Deactivation

If you're unsure about permanent deletion:

  • Contact us at support@fuseo.site to discuss temporary deactivation options
  • Deactivation may allow you to return to your account later
  • Your profile will be hidden during deactivation

5.7 Contact for Deletion Assistance

If you need help with account deletion:

  • Email: support@fuseo.site or privacy@fuseo.site
  • Subject: "Account Deletion Help" or "GDPR Deletion Request"
  • Response Time: Within 24-48 hours

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide the App's services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy.

Active Accounts:
  • Your data is retained while your account is active
  • You can update or delete content at any time
Deleted Accounts:
  • After you request account deletion, most data is removed within 30 days
  • Some information may remain in backup systems for up to 90 days
  • Certain information may be retained longer if required by law or for legitimate business purposes (fraud prevention, legal disputes, safety investigations)
Messages and Photos:
  • Chat messages are retained while both users maintain active accounts
  • Photos uploaded to Cloudinary are deleted when you delete your account or remove specific photos
  • Matched users may retain copies of messages in their chat history
Moderation Data:
  • Reports, blocks, and moderation actions are retained while your account is active
  • This data helps us maintain platform safety and identify patterns of abuse
  • Moderation data is deleted with your account (within 30 days) unless retention is required for ongoing investigations or legal compliance
Analytics Data:
  • Aggregated, anonymized usage data may be retained indefinitely for analytics purposes
  • This data cannot identify you personally

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Security Measures Include:
  • Encryption of data in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • Secure password storage using industry-standard hashing (bcrypt)
  • JWT (JSON Web Token) authentication for API requests
  • Secure database hosting with Supabase
  • Regular security updates and patches
  • Access controls limiting employee access to personal data
  • Secure cloud storage with Cloudinary
  • Regular security monitoring and threat detection
What We Cannot Guarantee:
  • No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure
  • While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security
  • You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 18 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at support@fuseo.site. We will take steps to delete such information from our systems.
